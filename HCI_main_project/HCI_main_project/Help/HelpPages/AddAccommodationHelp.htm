Help - Add/Edit Accommodation Tripago

Add/Edit Accommodation page is stepper made of 2 steps. Each step helps you to move towards new/edited accommodation.

Need help for a specific page part? Click to jump to specific help part:

  • Step 1
  • Step 2
  • Step 3

● Help - Step 1

Step 1 contains radio button for selecting accommodation type. After type is selected "Fill general info" (Ctrl+2) button will be enabled so you can proceed to the next step.

● Help - Step 2

Step 2 has field for accommodation name and "Upload other image" button"(2)(Ctrl+U) which opens dialog for choosing image from your computer. After all required parts of the step are finished "Fill address info" (Ctrl+3) button will be enabled so you can proceed to the next step.

● Help - Step 3

Step 3 has form for address information. You can fill form manually or just double-click on the map and we will do the rest. After every field is filled correctly "Create accommodation" (1.) button will be enabled so you can finish create/edit. Also you can go back to step 1 in every moment by clicking "Back to general info" (2.)(Ctrl+2) button or by clicking step number (3.) located in the top part of the page.
