Help - Add/Edit Tour Tripago

Add/Edit Tour page is stepper made of 2 steps. Each step helps you to move towards new/edited tour.

Need help for a specific page part? Click to jump to specific help part:

  • Step 1
  • Step 2
  • Drag and drop steps

● Help - Step 1

Step 1 has field for tour name and "Upload other image" button"(2)(Ctrl+U) which opens dialog for choosing image from your computer. After all required parts of the step are finished "Fill details info" (Ctrl+3) button will be enabled so you can proceed to the next step.

● Help - Step 2

Step 2 has form for details information. After every field is filled correctly "Select attractions" (1.)(Ctrl+3) button will be enabled so you can proceed to the next step.. Also you can go back to step 1 in every moment by clicking "Back to general info" (2.)(Ctrl+1) button or by clicking step number (3.) located in the top part of the page.

● Help - Drag and drop steps

Drag & drop is the way to choose attractions, accommodations and restaurants for tour. On the left side is the list of all attractions/accommodations/restaurants and on the right side is the list of specific ones for new tour. By dragging from left box and dropping to the right, attractions/accommodations/restaurants are being chosen. User can also drag from right box and drop the element to the left one. Button will be disabled until at least one element is placed in right box.
