Help - Homepage Tripago

Homepage is the main page of the application.
On the left there's the navbar which enables you to switch different views on the right, and choose if you want to see available Tours, Attractions, Accommodation, Restaurants, Tour history, or open Help window (like this one).
On the right is the card pane, with scrolling view of different types of cards on the bottom, and search & filter bar on the top.

● Help - Navbar

Navbar is the main source of navigation in the app. It allows you to choose from 4 different card display types:

  • Tours
  • Accommodation
  • Attractions
  • Restaurants

Selecting either one of these options will change the content and look (slightly) of the cards on the right, displaying information for the chosen entities.
Read more about different card displays here.

Right below those four type-oriented options, there's an option for you to open "Tour History".
This option will open up the history view on the right pane, showing all the tours you reserved and/or booked.
More on that here.

"Help" navbar option will showcase the same behaviour as pressing "F1" key on your keyboard will - opening the Help broswer window (just like this one), with documentation and explanation of the page in focus.

● Help - Cards display & options

There are two possible card display types: Tour Card and Simple Card. Here's how they map onto the four navbar selection options:

  • Tours - Tour Card
  • Accommodation - Simple Card
  • Attractions - Simple Card
  • Restaurants - Simple Card

This section is somewhat extensive, feel free to jump to the part you are interested in by choosing one of the topics:

  • Tour Card
  • Tour Card - Information Button
  • Simple Card

Tour Card

Tour Card is designated for displaying tours. It shows tour image, price, name, dates => basic information about each tour.

Tours are the whole planned trips our agents prepared just for you. Each tour includes different restaurants, accommodation and attractions you'll get to enjoy during the tour.
To see the whole tour plan and description, and get a chance to book/reserve a tour, click on the "Information" button on the bottom left - more on that (just like this one)

Tour Card - Information

Clicking on the "Information" button will open a window with detailed information about the tour. Besides the information on the card, tour description and tour attractions, accommodation and restaurant will show.
Handy Map view of all information will also be displayed - including color-coordinated markers for each of the accommodation/attractions/restaurants included, and the path connecting all attractions you'll get to explore during the trip.

To read more about tour information page, click "F1" or "?" button on the information page of your interest (while running the app).

Simple Card

Simple Card is designated for displaying attractions, accommodation, and restaurants - one card for all of them because they hold the same families of informations. It shows image, name, and address information of each item.

Unlike Tours, attractions, accommodation, and restaurants don't have much information about them - each of them has its address (Street, Street Number, Postal Code, City), and on top of that accommodation also has its type (hotel/apartment).

● Help - Tour History

Tour History navbar button will open the overview of all Tours you booked and/or reserved through the app.

It also displays a elongated version of Tour cards, with all the same infomation as before (in a bit of a different layout).

Each tour history card has a label in the bottom right corner indicating the status of your purchase. It can be:

  • booked - you booked the tour
  • reserved - you reserved the tour

*Reminder: "booking" a tour means paying and not beeing able to cancel the booking afterwards, while reservations don't require immediate purchase and can be later canceled.

If the tour status is "reserved" and the tour dates are in the future, you have the option to cancel the tour - a gray "Cancel reservation" button will be placed right before the status label.

To cancel a tour, simply click on the "Cancel reservation" button. Just to be sure, a "Are you sure you want to cancel" pop-up will appear, to prevent any accidents.
If you are sure, click on the blue "Cancel reservation" button, otherwise choose to "Cancel" and close the pop-up.

You can also click on any of the cards' "Information" button to see tour details from here as well, same bahviour as mentioned before.

● Help - Search

Search bar is located in the top right corner of the Homepage.

By typing text into the search bar, the cards displayed will change to include only the items with the name that contains the text typed in the search bar.

No need to click any buttons, just type and the list of items will filter with each written letter.

● Help - Sort

Sort drop down menu is located to the right of the Search bar. Sorting is only available for tours (both in the "Tours" and "Tour Statistics" view).

There are five sorting criteria:

  • Most popular - cards will be sorted by the number of reservations and bookings (in sum) so that the first tour shown is the "most popular"
  • Price lowest - cards will be sorted by the price so that the first tour shown is the the cheapest
  • Price highest - cards will be sorted by the price so that the first tour shown is the the priciest
  • Date desc - cards will be sorted by the the "from" date, so that the first tour shown starts the latest
  • Date asc - cards will be sorted by the the "from" date, so that the first tour shown starts the soonest

Default sorting option is "Most popular".

● Help - Filter

Filter button is located in the top right corner of the Homepage.

When you first open the app, the filters are hidden. To make them visible simply click on the filter button => filter options outlined with gray border container will appear:

Now you can choose to filter the items shown by different criteria, based on the item type:

  • Tours - filter by min and max price, as well as date range
  • Attractions - filter by attraction location (choose city option from the drop down menu)
  • Accommodation - filter by accommodation location (choose city option from the drop down menu), as well as accommodation type (choose type option from drom down menu)

When you're happy with the choices for filters you inputed, click on the "Apply filters" button to filter the card selection - only the cards who's content matches the filter inputs will remain.
Keep in mind: "Apply filters" button will be disabled (you won't be able to click it) unless the inputs you provided are valid - just follow error messages and make sure to correct the explained mistakes, and the button will enable after everything is corrected.

Filtering takes into consideration the search and sort values (if provided) so the final selection will contain cards that match all three criteria (given by search, sort and filters).

To clear all filters at the same time, press the "Clear filters" button - cards will update to include only the criteria left by search and sort (if they exist).
